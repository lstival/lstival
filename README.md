## 🔬 Research

I work at the intersection of **Self-Supervised Learning (SSL)** and **Explainability**, developing training paradigms that learn from large-scale unlabeled data while remaining interpretable.

My current focus areas:

- 🛰️ **Remote Sensing & Earth Observation** — representation learning for multispectral and spatiotemporal satellite data
- 🔎 **Explainable AI** — building models whose internal processes are transparent, not just accurate
- 🌿 **Green Life Sciences** — applying SSL to plant phenology and epidemiology at WUR
- 📈 **Time Series Modeling** — leveraging SSM (Mamba) and KAN architectures as Transformer alternatives
- 🎨 **Video Understanding** — colorization, temporal consistency, and self-supervised pretraining
